# Bug Report - FPS Drop in Specific Area

**ID:** BUG-008  
**Title:** Significant FPS drop in certain areas  
**Severity:** High  
**Priority:** Medium  
**Environment:** PC / Performance  

## Description
Frame rate drops significantly when entering specific areas of the map.

## Extended Description
This may be caused by unoptimized assets, lighting, or too many active objects. It affects gameplay smoothness and can impact player experience.

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Enter a specific area of the map
2. Monitor FPS

## Expected Result
Stable FPS across all areas.

## Actual Result
FPS drops noticeably, causing lag.

Open
