# Bug Report - Hit Registration Issue

**ID:** BUG-014  
**Game:** Murder Mystery 2  
**Title:** Knife hits do not register properly  
**Severity:** Critical  
**Priority:** High  
**Environment:** Roblox / Multiplayer  

## Description
Knife attacks sometimes fail to register even when clearly hitting a player.

## Extended Description
This creates unfair gameplay situations, especially in competitive rounds. Likely related to hit detection or server-client desync.

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Play as murderer
2. Attempt to eliminate another player
3. Observe hit detection

## Expected Result
Hit should register and eliminate the target.

## Actual Result
No damage or elimination occurs.

## Status
Open
