# Bug Report - Player Falls Through Platform

**ID:** BUG-011  
**Game:** Tower of Hell  
**Title:** Player randomly falls through platforms  
**Severity:** High  
**Priority:** High  
**Environment:** Roblox / Gameplay  

## Description
Players sometimes fall through platforms without any visible gap.

## Extended Description
This affects progression and fairness, especially in timed obstacle courses. The issue may be related to collision detection or server lag.

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Play a Tower of Hell round
2. Jump between platforms
3. Observe character behavior

## Expected Result
Player should land safely on platforms.

## Actual Result
Player falls through solid surfaces.

## Status
Open
